About The Author

Dr. Arun Kumar Rawal, PT

MSK & Sports Physiotherapist

Pelvic Girdle Pain Specialist (USA)

Dr. Arun is a highly respected senior physiotherapist with over 12 years of experience in orthopedic and sports injury rehabilitation. A graduate of MS Ramaiah Physiotherapy College, he is certified in Pelvic Girdle Rehabilitation by Richard Jackson, USA.

As the leader of the multidisciplinary team at PREHAB | Musculoskeletal & Sports Health Clinics, Dr. Arun specializes in fast recovery for athletes and non-athletes, focusing on return-to-play strategies.
