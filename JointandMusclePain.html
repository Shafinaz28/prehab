Complete Physiotherapy Service Details

Consult a specialized musculoskeletal and sports physiotherapist for expert assessment, consultation, and rehabilitation.

YOU CAN CONSULT FOR:

  • Lower back pain: Disc injury, sciatica, and nerve pain
  • Neck pain: Cervical spondylosis, postural pain, and stiffness
  • Knee pain: Chondromalacia patellae, patellofemoral pain syndrome, and related issues
  • Shoulder pain: Frozen shoulder, rotator cuff pain, and post-dislocation rehabilitation
  • Sports and ligament injuries: Ankle sprains, ACL injuries, and more
  • Post-surgical rehabilitation: TKR, ACL, spine surgery, fractures, and more
  • Arthritis: Osteoarthritis and rheumatoid arthritis
  • Posture-related pain: Pain caused by prolonged sitting or desk work
  • Guided home exercise support: Structured recovery exercises for safe progress
